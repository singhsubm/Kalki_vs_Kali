Kalki, the final avatar of Vishnu

Kali Purush is an embodiment of darkness and chaos in Hindu mythology. Kalki is a prophesied future warrior-avatar of Vishnu in Hinduism, destined to restore order and righteousness at the end of the current age.

KALI YUGA

Kali Purusha represents the chaotic and destructive aspects of the cosmos, while Kalki Avatar is a future warrior-avatar of Vishnu expected to restore order and righteousness during a time of great upheaval and moral decline.

Hanuman

One of the greatest brahmachari, he served Rama. He is the most ardent vanara devotee of Rama.

Parashurama

The sixth avatar of Vishnu. He is knowledgeable in all astras, shastras, and divine weapons.

Vyasa

The sage who composed the Mahabharata. He represents erudition and wisdom.

Vibhishana

The brother of Ravana. Vibhishana defected to Rama before his battle with Ravana.

Ashwatthama

He would live forever but with his body covered with painful sores and ulcers that would never be cured

Mahabali

The ruler of the asuras, he is still revered in present-day Kerala. His son was Banasura

Kripa

The royal guru of the princes in the Mahabharata. He was adopted by King Shantanu.

Markandeya

A revered sage in Hindu mythology, known for his deep devotion and timeless wisdom.
